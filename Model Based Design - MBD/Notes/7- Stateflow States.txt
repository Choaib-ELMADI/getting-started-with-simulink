. A state describes an operating mode of a reactive system.
    -> Each state describes a stable stable of the system.
    -> The state name shall correspond to the aim of the state.

. Exclusive (OR) States:
    -> In exclusive states, only one state is active at a given time.
    -> To switch between OR and AND states:
        > Enter the Chart -> Right Click -> Decomposition -> ...

. Parallel (AND) States:
    -> In parallel states, states are executed in 'pseudo' parallel.
    -> To switch between AND and OR states:
        > Enter the Chart -> Right Click -> Decomposition -> ...
