. Stateflow data scope:
    -> Input     : Read-only    , Comes from Simulink inputs
    -> Output    : Write-only   , Sent to Simulink outputs
    -> Local     : Read & Write , Internal to Stateflow
    -> Parameter : Read-only	, Defined in Simulink & used in Stateflow
    -> Constant  : Read-only	, Internal to Stateflow
